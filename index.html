Why Choose Us

We create spaces that maximize comfort for modern and traditional lifestyles. Everyone deserves to feel good, Our designs blend aesthetics, functionality, and affordability, bringing your dreams to life. Welcome to a world of thoughtfully designed spaces that elevate your well-being.

Visionary Approach

Our young team brings a fresh outlook to every project, fostering innovation and ingenuity in our designs.

Tailored Solutions

We understand that each client's needs are unique. Hence, we offer personalized solutions that resonate with their aspirations.

Emphasis on Quality

Quality is non-negotiable for us. We prioritize excellence in design, materials, and craftsmanship to deliver nothing but the best.

Professionalism

Despite our youthful energy, we maintain the utmost professionalism in our dealings, ensuring timely delivery and adherence to project timelines.

Services

The Studio 162 team has vast experience in the following sectors: residential, offices, retail, hotel, education, healthcare, business parks, and mixed-use developments. The firm offers architectural, master planning, urban planning, and interior design services. The crew has years of expertise working on several projects across India. By aiming for universal human appeal, the firm's architecture transcends national boundaries. The company is committed to exceeding clients' expectations in terms of design, performance, and service. We want to combine design quality with economic feasibility. Our unconventional approach has resulted in novel solutions.

Architecture Design

We take pride in conceptualizing and crafting designs that not only reflect our clients' aspirations but also emphasize functionality, aesthetics, and sustainability. From commercial edifices to residential dwellings, each project is meticulously tailored to suit the unique requirements of our valued clientele.

Interior Design

Our expertise extends beyond external structures. With a keen eye for detail and a focus on creating harmonious spaces, our interior design team transforms buildings into inviting and functional environments that stand the test of time.

Consultancy

We understand that navigating the complexities of architectural endeavours can be daunting. As seasoned consultants, we offer invaluable guidance, ensuring our clients' projects adhere to regulations, align with industry best practices, and meet their specific objectives.

Renovation and Adaptive Reuse

Breathing new life into existing structures by transforming them into modern, functional spaces that align with today's needs and design trends.
